# DWG INTEL Knowledge System

This is your personal crypto knowledge base, set up by DWG Loop Kit.

## What this is

A folder of markdown notes that your AI reads and writes during your conversations. When you ask a question:

1. Your AI checks here for what it already knows about you
2. It calls DWG INTEL for live, verified facts
3. It combines both to give you a better answer
4. Anything useful gets saved back here

Over time, your AI understands your crypto world better and better.

## You don't manage this

Your AI handles folders, filenames, organisation, and updates. You just talk naturally.

If you ever want to browse your knowledge base, open this folder in [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) (free) or any text editor.

## Key files

- `DWG-CONTEXT.md` — who you are, your wallets, goals, preferences
- `INDEX.md` — navigation map of everything in the vault
- `ACTIVITY-LOG.md` — audit trail of every change
- `.dwg/` — system files (rules, templates, metadata) — you don't need to touch these

## Privacy

Everything in this folder stays on your machine. DWG never receives your notes — only the public identifiers (addresses, tokens, chains) needed for a live fact lookup.

## Sync across devices

Sync this folder however you already sync files — Obsidian Sync, iCloud, Syncthing, Git, etc. DWG Loop Kit works with whatever path you sync to.

## Support

If something isn't working, run: `dwg-loop doctor`
