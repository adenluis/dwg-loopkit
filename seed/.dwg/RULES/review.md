# Knowledge Review Rules

> Condensed from DWG INTEL Prompt 4. Read this when the member says "run my knowledge review" or similar.

---

## Purpose

Review the member's knowledge base and protect its quality over time. You are a research curator, not a vault cleaner. An imperfect but useful vault beats a perfectly organised one that wastes the member's time.

After each review the knowledge system should be: more accurate, more current, better connected, easier to retrieve from, more honest about uncertainty, clearer about what needs attention.

---

## How to start

1. Read `DWG-CONTEXT.md`, `INDEX.md`, and `ACTIVITY-LOG.md`
2. Search the vault for `#dwg/flag` markers and process them
3. Check `Daily Notes/` and any notes marked `#inbox` — file quick captures into their proper homes
4. Review recently updated knowledge, active projects, active watchlists, current research

Do not begin by scanning the entire vault. Build context first.

---

## Scale the review

- **Small vault:** review everything
- **Medium/large vault:** prioritise recently changed knowledge, active projects, unfinished research, and the next section not reviewed recently

Over time every part gets attention. **Never claim full coverage unless you actually reviewed everything.**

---

## What to look for

| Area | Questions |
|---|---|
| **Accuracy** | Is it still true? Has newer info replaced it? Is the source still reliable? |
| **Completeness** | Is important context missing? Would a future conversation understand this topic? |
| **Retrieval** | Would you naturally find this note when answering future questions? Improve connections if not. |
| **Trust** | Can claims be traced to evidence? Are sources and dates present? Are facts vs opinions clear? |
| **Evolution** | Has the member's thinking changed? Has research evolved? Have questions been answered? |
| **Contradictions** | Conflicts between facts, theses, assumptions, decisions, or research conclusions |

---

## Safe improvements (automatic)

- Add helpful links between related knowledge
- Repair obvious broken links
- Add missing sources when already known
- Record review dates
- Mark information as needing verification or potentially stale
- Improve retrieval where no meaning changes

## Requires member approval

- Rewriting the member's opinions
- Merging notes
- Archiving notes
- Deleting notes
- Changing investment theses
- Replacing historical conclusions
- Reorganising the vault

---

## Review summary

Finish every review with a short plain-English summary:

### Overall health
Healthy / mostly healthy with areas to review / needs attention.

### What changed
Safe improvements made, flags and quick captures cleared.

### What needs attention
Items requiring the member's approval. Short, prioritised.

### Research worth revisiting
Unfinished work, unanswered questions, stale knowledge, promising areas. Framed as opportunities, never actions.

### Coverage
What you reviewed and what remains for future reviews. Be honest.

---

## After the review

1. Update `.dwg/METADATA.md` — set `last_review: YYYY-MM-DD`
2. Add `ACTIVITY-LOG.md` entry for the review
3. If the member approves any structural changes, execute them with the normal write tier rules

---

## What belongs elsewhere

This review **maintains** knowledge. It does not:
- Perform long-term reflection (that's `reflection.md`)
- Capture new knowledge (that's `capture.md`)
- Give trading advice or ratings (never)

---

## Success measure

One question: **Will future conversations be noticeably better because of this review?**

If yes, the review was successful.
